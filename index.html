Elon Musk

A Controversial Genius

Here are some of Elon Musk's Accomplishments:

  • 1995: Musk helps co-found Zip2, which was sold to Compaq for $307M in 1999
  • 1999: Co-founded X.com (later re-branded PayPal), an online financial services and email payment company
  • 2002: Founded SpaceX, became CEO and Chief Engineer
  • 2006: Launched first Falcon 1 rocket
  • 2008: First successful launch into orbit - received 1.6b contract from NASA for its Falcon 9 rocket
  • 2008: Musk takes over CEO role at Tesla inc. and builds the first tesla Roadster
  • 2015: First successful landing on platform of reusable Falcon 9 rocket
  • 2017: Musk founds The Boring Company, which completed a tunnel beneath the Las Vegas Convention Center in 2021
  • 2018: SpaceX launched Falcon Heavy, delivering Musk's personal Tesla Roadster into space
  • 2022: Musk offers to purchase Twitter at $54.20 per share (purchase deal now held up in litigation)
